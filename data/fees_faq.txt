What are the semester fees for undergraduate courses?
The semester fees for undergraduate courses are ₹1,07,000 per semester. This includes tuition fees, library fees, and laboratory fees.

When is the fee payment deadline?
Fee payment deadline is typically 15 days after the semester begins. Late fees of ₹500 per day apply after the deadline.

How can I pay my fees?
You can pay fees through:
1. Online payment portal on the college website
2. Bank draft in favor of "College Name"
3. Cash payment at the accounts office (only during office hours)

What documents are needed for fee payment?
You need to bring:
- Student ID card
- Previous semester mark sheet
- Fee receipt from previous semester

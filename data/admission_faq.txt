What is the admission process for new students?
New student admission involves:
1. Online application submission
2. Entrance test (if applicable)
3. Document verification
4. Fee payment
5. Class allocation

What documents are required for admission?
Required documents include:
- 10th and 12th mark sheets
- Transfer certificate
- Conduct certificate
- Passport size photographs (6 copies)
- Caste certificate (if applicable)

When do classes start?
Classes typically start in the first week of July for the academic year. Exact dates are announced on the college website.
